Tandem Hydraulic Brake Circuit

BAJA SAE Physics Reference Model & Interactive Simulator

System Architecture

This reference implementation models a dual-circuit (tandem) hydraulic braking system designed for light off-road vehicles (BAJA SAE). The system splits pressure from a single foot pedal into two isolated fluid circuits to maintain vehicle control under varying friction and weight transfer conditions.

Front Circuit (Direct)

Connected directly to the secondary master cylinder piston. Feeds two twin-piston calipers at full system pressure for maximum stopping power as weight shifts forward under deceleration.

Rear Circuit (Proportioned)

Feeds single-piston rear calipers through a proportioning valve. Above a critical knee pressure (25 bar), rear line pressure gain is capped to prevent premature rear-wheel lockup.

Thermal & Friction Dynamics

Calculates kinetic power dissipation across both axles. Rotor temperatures accumulate heat and cool toward ambient air, dynamically reducing pad friction coefficient (μ) when temperatures exceed 250°C.
